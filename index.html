Budget
The budget feature allows you to define biweekly, monthly, weekly income and expenses. You have the ability to specify the account income will be deposited into or expenses will be withdrawn from.
Calendar
The calendar gives you a monthly summary of your income and expenses broken down by account and presents them visually for you on a calendar so you know which bills are due when and remind you when payments are due to you from others.

Privacy and Security
Protecting your financial data from unauthorized access is a top priority. Here are the steps we take to protect your financial data.
  • Your financial data is encrypted at rest with a non-default Amazon Web Services KMS key.
  • All connections are SSL encrypted with a certificate from Amazon Web Services
  • Amazon Web Services Cognito is used for authentication with multi-factor authentication required
  • Your bank data is never stored on our servers. The bank data shown in the application is transient. The only data related to your bank held on our servers is an access token to connect to your bank.
  • Our software never has direct access to your bank's username and password. Our bank integrations are built with a well known and trusted third party Plaid which manages the bank login process ensuring it's secure.
